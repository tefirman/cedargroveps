## Our Programs

Cedar Grove Public Schools offers a variety of programs designed to meet the diverse needs and interests of our students.

### Core Academic Program

Our core curriculum covers:

- **English Language Arts**: Reading, writing, speaking, and listening skills
- **Mathematics**: Building strong foundational skills and mathematical thinking
- **Science**: Hands-on exploration and scientific inquiry
- **Social Studies**: Understanding our world, history, and civic responsibility

### Specialized Programs

#### STEM Initiative
Our STEM program integrates science, technology, engineering, and mathematics through hands-on projects and real-world problem solving.

#### Arts Integration
We believe in the power of the arts to enhance learning across all subjects. Students engage in visual arts, music, and performance.

#### Special Education Services
We provide comprehensive support for students with diverse learning needs, including individualized education programs (IEPs) and targeted interventions.

#### English Language Learner (ELL) Support
Our ELL program supports students in developing English proficiency while maintaining access to rigorous academic content.

### Extended Learning

- After-school tutoring and enrichment
- Summer learning programs
- Field trips and experiential learning opportunities
